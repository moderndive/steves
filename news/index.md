# Changelog

## steves 0.1.1

- Added attribution and a clarifying note across the README, dataset
  documentation, `DESCRIPTION`, and the get-started vignette: the
  dataset is compiled from public sources for teaching purposes, is not
  an official or verified Rick Steves’ Europe dataset, and is shared
  with the permission of the Rick Steves’ Europe team.

## steves 0.1.0

- Initial release.
- Adds `episodes`, a tibble of every *Rick Steves’ Europe* episode aired
  2000–2025 (159 rows × 38 columns) covering identity, editorial fields,
  geography, air dates, IMDB ratings, country flags, thumbnails, and
  canonical best-of summaries.
- Includes two vignettes: “Get started with steves” and “Mapping the
  show”.
