# steves 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* Adds `episodes`, a tibble of every *Rick Steves' Europe* episode aired
  2000–2025 (159 rows × 38 columns) covering identity, editorial fields,
  geography, air dates, IMDB ratings, country flags, thumbnails, and
  canonical best-of summaries.
* Includes two vignettes: "Get started with steves" and "Mapping the show".
